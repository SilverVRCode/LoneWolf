Welcome to Lone Wolf,

a game about a wolf who gets separated from his pack.

Lone Wolf is a hackathon game created for the MasseyHacks X hackathon.

How did it start?

To the left, you see our old logo.

MasseyHacks X of 2024 was coming up, and I started thinking of this idea.

A lone wolf who was separated from his pack. A great idea that would truly create something beautiful

By the time (most of you) are seeing this, Lone Wolf is released and available to download.

Download Lone Wolf

Lone Wolf currently only supports Windows, however, we are working on ports for more devices.
